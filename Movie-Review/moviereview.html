Barbie

Margot Robbie, Ryan Gosling

Barbie is colorful, clever, and surprisingly emotional. Margot Robbie shines in a role that balances fun with depth. It’s a movie about identity, expectations, and pink — lots of pink. Smart, funny, and worth the hype.

The Marvels

Kamala Khan

A fast, fun Marvel adventure with solid team chemistry. Kamala Khan (Iman Vellani) brings most of the charm. The plot moves quickly, sometimes too quickly, but it’s light and entertaining. Great for a fun movie night.

Wish

Chris Pine, Ariana Debose

A classic Disney-style story with a modern look and message. Asha is a strong and likable lead, and the songs are uplifting. The animation is unique and visually sweet. It’s not groundbreaking, but it’s warm and magical.
